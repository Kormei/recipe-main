Fish and Chips Recipe

Description

Fish and chips is a classic British dish consisting of deep-fried battered fish served with golden, crispy fries. Often accompanied by tartar sauce, lemon wedges, and sometimes mushy peas, it’s a comforting and iconic meal.

Ingredients

  • White fish fillets (like cod or haddock)
  • Potatoes for fries
  • Flour for batter
  • Beer or sparkling water (for batter)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil for frying
  • Optional: tartar sauce, lemon wedges

Steps

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into fries, then soak in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix flour, salt, and beer (or sparkling water) to make the batter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Pat fish dry, dip in batter, and carefully fry until golden and crispy.
  5. Drain fish on paper towels. Fry potatoes until golden and crispy.
  6. Serve fish and chips with tartar sauce and lemon wedges.
