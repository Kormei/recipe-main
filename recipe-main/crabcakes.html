Crab Cakes Recipe

Description

Crab cakes are a classic seafood dish made from fresh crab meat, breadcrumbs, and seasonings, shaped into patties and pan-fried or baked until golden brown. They are often served with tartar sauce, lemon wedges, or a side salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh crab meat
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Old Bay seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter or oil for frying
  • Optional: lemon wedges, tartar sauce for serving

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine crab meat, breadcrumbs, egg, mayonnaise, mustard, and seasonings.
  2. Gently mix until ingredients are combined, being careful not to break up the crab too much.
  3. Form mixture into patties of desired size.
  4. Heat butter or oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  5. Cook crab cakes 3–4 minutes per side until golden brown and heated through.
  6. Serve hot with lemon wedges and tartar sauce.
